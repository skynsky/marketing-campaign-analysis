# Marketing Campaign Data Analysis

## Overview

This project analyzes marketing campaign data to understand customer behavior, purchasing patterns, and campaign effectiveness.

## Business Objective

The goal is to identify customer characteristics and determine factors that influence campaign success in order to improve marketing strategies.

## Tools Used

* Python
* Pandas
* NumPy
* Matplotlib
* Jupyter Notebook

## Data Preparation

* Handled missing values
* Removed duplicate records
* Converted data types
* Standardized categorical variables

## Exploratory Data Analysis

### Customer Profile Analysis

* Age distribution
* Income distribution
* Education level
* Marital status

### Purchase Behavior

* Product spending analysis
* Total customer expenditure
* Purchase frequency

### Campaign Performance

* Campaign acceptance rates
* Customer response analysis
* High-value customer identification

## Key Insights

* Customer income strongly influences spending behavior.
* A small group of customers contributes a significant portion of revenue.
* Campaign response rates vary across customer segments.
* Certain demographic groups are more likely to engage with promotions.

## Business Recommendations

* Focus campaigns on high-value customer segments.
* Personalize promotions based on customer demographics.
* Improve targeting to increase campaign conversion rates.
